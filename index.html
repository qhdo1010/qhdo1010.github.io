The Hidden Neuron

Hi, I am a neuroscience PhD student who is frustrated with how dense scientific papers can be and am starting this blog to write about cool papers on cool topics in neuroscience that I enjoy, in hope of opening up the field to the more casual readers. Honestly though, I am mostly doing this to help myself get a better grasp of the materials, cause I am struggling to keep up with all these papers that I have to read for lab, and I realize that I learn best when I try to explain stuff to other people in my own words.
